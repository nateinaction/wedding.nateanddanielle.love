The Venue

The Charles Page Zilker Clubhouse is part of Austin's iconic 351-acre Zilker Park. Designed by Charles Page, the limestone structure was built in 1934 by the Civil Works Administration (CWA) for the local Boy Scouts group. Sitting atop the highest peak of Zilker Park, the Clubhouse provides breathtaking views of Downtown Austin’s skyline.

Parking is available on site for approximately 50 cars. Please carpool to ensure enough space for everyone. Parking outside of the gate is prohibited and subject to towing.

Smoking is not allowed anywhere on the property.
